How do I prevent getting hacked?

+

There are many things you can do to prevent getting hacked. Use a random password generator or random, imperfect sentence (ex. &AzebraisaAstripedfunhorseIthinkitsCUTE&) Be careful where you click! Be on the lookout for phishing scams. Only make online purchases from reputable vendors wuth website URL https://. Turn on multifactor identification. Change passwords regularly. Don't use the same password for multiple logins. Better yet, use a password manager. Keep your apps and OS updated. Only download reputable apps from app store or Google Play; check reviews. Delete old accounts you no longer use. Encrypt the data on your phone. Turn off bluetooth when not in use.
